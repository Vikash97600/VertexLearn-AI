Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common inquiries regarding the platform's foundation.

The AI Tutor uses a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipeline built with FastAPI and pgvector. When a course video is uploaded, its transcript and notes are chunked and converted into vector embeddings. When a student queries, the system finds matching vector chunks from that specific course and presents it to the Groq LLM model to generate grounded answers with source citations.
